The basis of the technology of "Biofitonov®" the principle cryo grinding - maximum grinding plant raw materials at low temperatures (about -1960C), so that opened a plant cell membrane (the principle cryomechanical plant activation).

low processing temperature does not allow the loss of essential oils, substances are destroyed by heating, it prevents natural oxidation. cryotech- also includes the effect of shock freezing, which allows you to stop the biochemical processes in the cell without disturbing them.

Cryo grinding provides recovery of all classes of substances (the water-soluble, fat-soluble, alcohol-soluble) are simultaneously and without the use of solvents, as opposed to traditional technologies.
